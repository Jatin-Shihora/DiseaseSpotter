Be your own doctor with the help of disease spotter...

It may have occurred so many times that you or someone you know requires quick medical attention, but they are unavailable for various reasons. The Disease Prediction system is a project for end-user assistance and online consultation. In this paper, we present a web application that uses an intelligent system to provide users with real-time advice on their ailment. The application receives numerous details as well as the disease connected with those details. The software allows users to communicate their disease-related concerns.It then examines user-specific information to look for illnesses that may be related with it. In this case, we employ clever machine learning algorithms to estimate the most correct ailment that may be linked to the patient's information.
